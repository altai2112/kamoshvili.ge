kamoshvili

Maxim Kamoshvili was born on June 21, 1985, in the Grauerman Maternity Hospital, to a family with Georgian origins and traditions.

The Kamoshvili family home in Tbilisi (from personal archive)

Since 2005, Kamoshvili has worked at academic institutions developing methods for aerospace monitoring of the Earth. He also worked on popular science projects.

In 2021, due to personal circumstances, he left to live and work in the European Jerusalem - Sarajevo, where he was engaged in research projects and artistic practices.

In collaboration with representatives of European conceptualism, such as Gianfranco Baruchello, Emilio Fantin, and others, Kamoshvili created a number of theoretical and artistic works.

Returning to Moscow, Kamoshvili presented some of the works he created in Europe as part of the exhibition "Celebration Celebration."

Kamoshvili's artistic practices question the structure of art, meaning, and narrative—an open invitation to reflect on the absurd side of the artistic system and other related systems.
